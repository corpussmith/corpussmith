Premium beta

Six seams on top of freemium

Premium adds structured analysis across the corpus you already harvested. It never replaces or paywalls freemium functionality — it layers on top.

  • Atlas — citation / co-citation graph of your corpus.
  • Clusters — topic clustering with auto-labels.
  • Contradictions — surface opposing claims about the same subject.
  • Thesis pack — chapter outline, literature matrix, per-chapter bibliographies.
  • Book pack — long-form scaffold seeded by clusters.
  • Memory graph — persistent, cross-project research graph.

Premium seams are present in v3.4.0-beta.1 and raise PremiumNotAvailableError until activated.

Pricing is still being set. Expected: a flat one-time licence, plus optional annual updates. No subscription lock-in, no usage metering.

For institutions

Departmental and library licences are planned once the premium paid build ships. If you represent a research group or institution and want an early conversation, email me.

Why not a subscription?

Scholars already pay subscriptions to more tools than any person should have to remember. Corpus Smith is terminal software that runs on your machine, against public APIs. A one-time licence fits the shape of the tool.
