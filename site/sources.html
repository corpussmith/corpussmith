Trust labels

Every returned record is classified into one of four trust tiers:

  • peer-reviewed — published in a peer-reviewed venue with editorial oversight.
  • peer-or-preprint — either peer-reviewed or a preprint from a credible repository.
  • community — open repository, grey literature, report, or dataset.
  • unclassified — metadata insufficient for confident classification.
