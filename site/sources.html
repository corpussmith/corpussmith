Trust labels

Every returned record is classified into one of four trust tiers:

Peer-reviewed

Published in a venue with editorial oversight and formal peer review.

Peer-or-preprint

Either peer-reviewed or a preprint from a credible repository (arXiv, SSRN, etc.).

Community

Open repository, grey literature, report, or dataset.

Unclassified

Metadata insufficient for confident classification.
